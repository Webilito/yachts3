About Lagoon 42

The Lagoon 42 is a modern catamaran that offers exceptional stability, space, and comfort. With its innovative design and dual-hull configuration, this vessel provides a smooth sailing experience even in rougher conditions, making it perfect for families or groups seeking a relaxing cruise.

Unlike monohull sailboats, the Lagoon 42's wide beam creates spacious living areas both inside and out. The large cockpit and foredeck lounging areas are ideal for socializing, sunbathing, or enjoying meals with panoramic ocean views.
