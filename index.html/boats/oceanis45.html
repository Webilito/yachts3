About Oceanis 45

The Oceanis 45 is a sailing yacht that combines excellent performance with comfort and spaciousness. This elegant vessel offers a perfect balance of sailing pleasure and relaxation, making it ideal for families or groups of friends looking to explore coastal waters.

With its modern hull design, the Oceanis 45 sails efficiently even in light winds. The cockpit is designed for easy handling, allowing even those with limited sailing experience to enjoy the thrill of controlling this magnificent yacht.
