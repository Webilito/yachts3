Our Story

Founded in 2010, Fly Tours began with a simple vision: to provide unforgettable yacht experiences along the world's most beautiful coastlines. What started as a small operation with a single yacht has grown into a premier yacht charter company with a diverse fleet and a team of experienced professionals.

Our journey has been marked by a commitment to excellence, innovation, and customer satisfaction. We've expanded our services, refined our offerings, and built lasting relationships with clients who return year after year for their maritime adventures.

Our Mission

At Fly Tours, we believe that travel should be both luxurious and authentic. Our mission is to create personalized yacht experiences that allow our clients to discover hidden gems while enjoying the comfort and privacy of their own vessel.

Safety First

We maintain the highest safety standards and ensure all our vessels are equipped with the latest safety equipment.

Luxury Experience

Our yachts are designed for comfort and style, offering premium amenities and exceptional service.

Expert Navigation

Our experienced captains know the best routes and hidden spots to make your journey truly special.

Our Commitment

We are committed to sustainable tourism and environmental responsibility. Our yachts are equipped with eco-friendly systems, and we follow strict environmental guidelines to minimize our impact on the marine ecosystem.

We also believe in giving back to the communities we operate in, supporting local businesses and participating in marine conservation efforts.
