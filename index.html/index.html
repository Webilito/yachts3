About Tours Yacht Adventures

Founded in 2010, Fly Tours has been providing unforgettable yacht experiences along the world's most beautiful coastlines. Our team of experienced sailors and travel professionals are dedicated to creating personalized journeys that exceed your expectations.

We believe that travel should be both luxurious and authentic, allowing you to discover hidden gems while enjoying the comfort and privacy of your own yacht. Our fleet is meticulously maintained, and our crews are trained to provide exceptional service.
